# 001: Hero leads with mission, not pricing

## Decision

The hero section states who we are and what we do. It does not mention pricing, timelines, or competitive comparisons.

## Why

Pricing ("6 weeks, 3 months") is a sales argument, not an identity. The hero should answer "who are these people?" before "what do they cost?"

## Aligned to

`docs/mission.md`: "We accelerate software development through AI"

## Changes

- Earlier version had a pricing block ("They quote 6 months. We deliver in 6 weeks. You pay for 3 months."). Removed.
- Added "for teams across engineering, operations, and beyond" to signal scope beyond pure software development.

## What we considered but didn't do

- Leading with the racing car analogy. Too playful for a first impression. CEOs need to understand what we do before they enjoy a metaphor.
- Leading with a bold claim ("10x faster"). Can't back it up yet. Honesty first.
