# 006: Who we are for credibility

## Decision

Added a "Who we are" section describing the collective model, experience level, and tone.

## Why

Zero social proof means zero trust. Even without case studies or logos, stating "senior engineers with years of experience across industries" establishes baseline credibility. The collective model (not an agency, not a solo freelancer) is distinctive and worth explaining.

## Aligned to

`docs/how-we-work.md`: "collective of freelance developers and consultants who come together as a professional unit"

## Content

Three paragraphs:
1. What we are: a collective of senior engineers and consultants, AI-native approach
2. How we work: come together per engagement, small and fast, experienced and trusted
3. The tone: playful name, serious work, genuine enjoyment

## What we considered but didn't do

- Individual team member profiles. Too early. The collective model means the roster may vary per engagement.
- Years of experience or specific numbers. Only add when they're meaningful and verifiable.
- Client logos. Don't have them yet. Will add when we do.
