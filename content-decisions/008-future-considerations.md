# 008: Future considerations

Things to revisit as the business grows. None of these are decisions yet.

## Case studies

As we complete engagements, add real examples with permission. Numbers, timelines, outcomes. This is the strongest trust signal possible. Prioritize over any other content addition.

## Blog / thinking

Thought leadership from principles.md ("Innovation as a Habit"). Publish how we work, what we learn. Builds SEO and credibility. Consider a simple markdown-based blog within Next.js.

## Non-IT examples

As we work with non-engineering teams, add those stories. Broadens the audience beyond the current dev-heavy framing.

## Pricing page

Market research shows almost nobody publishes prices. Could be a differentiator through radical transparency. Decide after first engagements establish price anchors.

## Testimonials

Collect from day one. Even informal quotes from early clients. A single honest sentence from a real person beats any amount of marketing copy.

## Multi-language

German market is primary. Consider a DE version when content stabilizes. Don't translate until the English version is settled.

## Visual identity

Content-first approach is deliberate. Brand design comes after the message is right. Don't let design drive content. When ready: keep it clean, confident, not corporate.

## Individual profiles

When the collective grows and the roster stabilizes, add team member profiles. For now the collective description is sufficient.
