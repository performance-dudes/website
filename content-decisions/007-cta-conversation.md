# 007: CTA is a conversation, not a form

## Decision

End the page with "Let's talk" and an email address. No contact form, no booking calendar, no "request a demo."

## Why

Every page needs a next step. Ours is a conversation. The tone of the entire site is direct and human. A contact form or booking widget would break that.

"Got a project, an idea, or just a question?" keeps the bar low. No commitment implied.

## Contact

Email: contact@performance-dudes.de

## What we considered but didn't do

- Calendly or similar booking link. Too transactional. May add later when volume justifies it.
- Contact form. Adds complexity, needs a backend or service, and feels corporate.
- Phone number. May add for specific markets or when team size supports it.
