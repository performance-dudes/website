# 005: Customer journey section

## Decision

Added a "How it works" section showing four steps: We talk. We scope it. We build it. You own it.

## Why

A CEO who's convinced by the first three sections needs to know: "What happens if I say yes?" Without this, the page is inspiring but not actionable. Removing friction between interest and conversation is the goal.

## Aligned to

`docs/how-we-work.md`: "deliver, learn, improve, repeat"

## Design choices

- Four steps, not more. Each one sentence bold, one sentence explanation.
- "We talk" first. No pitch deck, no sales cycle. Signals low friction.
- "You own it" last. Reinforces the independence message from the values section.
- No mention of timelines or pricing in this section. That's for the offer, not the website.

## What we considered but didn't do

- A visual timeline or numbered steps. Too formal for the tone. The bold/explanation pattern flows better.
- Including "discovery phase" as a separate step. Too detailed for the website level. That's offer-level specificity.
