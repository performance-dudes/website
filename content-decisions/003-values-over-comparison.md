# 003: Values over comparison

## Decision

Replaced the "Us vs. them" comparison table with a "What we believe" section drawn from our principles. Also folded "How we're different" into this section.

## Why

Talking about competitors is defensive. Talking about values is confident. A reader who's been burned by consultancies will recognize what we're offering without us naming the problem.

Two sections were removed:
- "Us vs. them" comparison table
- "How we're different" (still comparative framing, just softer)

Both replaced by four value statements:
1. Speed is removing what slows you down
2. Knowledge belongs in the project, not in people's heads
3. AI generates. Humans judge.
4. We succeed when your team succeeds

## Aligned to

`docs/principles.md`: speed over ceremony, knowledge in the project, help others adopt, AI first

## What we considered but didn't do

- Keeping a subtle comparison ("Unlike traditional approaches..."). Still comparative. Cut it entirely.
- A separate "Our principles" page. Too much for now. The values section covers it. Can revisit when content grows.
